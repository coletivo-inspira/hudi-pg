# HUDI Pages interface system

## Product character

HUDI Pages is a practical creation tool, not a marketing-only landing page. The
first screen names the product and leads directly to the portfolio builder. The
interface should feel precise, modern, human and trustworthy.

## Brand foundations

- Primary: `#1E3A8A`
- Action: `#3B82F6`
- Highlight: `#60A5FA`
- Neutral: `#64748B`
- Canvas: `#F8FAFC`
- Typography: Inter, weights 400, 500, 600 and 700
- Composition: roughly 60 percent canvas, 30 percent primary, 10 percent action

## Geometry and depth

- Use straight corners and controlled overlap in the HUDI mark.
- Use an 8px maximum radius for tools and repeated cards.
- The builder is a working surface with clear borders, not a collection of
  decorative cards.
- Translucency is reserved for HUDI-branded blueprint surfaces. Avoid heavy
  shadows, blur or atmospheric decoration.

## Motion

- Motion explains assembly: blocks enter, the blueprint grid drifts and progress
  grows when the user changes steps.
- Keep interaction feedback between 140ms and 240ms.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` and preserve all functionality without motion.

## Accessibility

- Keep a visible keyboard focus ring.
- Every form control must have a label.
- Do not communicate progress, theme or submission status by color alone.
- Maintain a minimum 44px target for primary interactive controls.
